Frequently Asked Questions

What airtime can be used?

AirtimeFlip now accepts accepts all networks, which includes MTN, Airtel, Glo, and 9Mobile.

How do I convert airtime to cash?

Using our online converter, it is very easy.

What is the minimum and maximum airtime amount accepted?

This varies for all four networks. The lowest airtime amount you can sell to us here is ₦ 750 for MTN, and ₦ 1000 for Airtel, 9Mobile and Glo.

What are the fees?

We charge only 19% fee on each MTN airtime transaction. So we will send ₦ 810 into your bank account if you convert ₦ 1000 airtime (19%), and so on. This is the cheapest offer available in Nigeria at the moment. Check our Charges page to see others.

When will I receive the cash?

The money will be sent into your bank account once we receive your airtime.
